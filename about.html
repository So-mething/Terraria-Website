Dig

In Terraria, every world is unique! Forage and mine for materials to create new gear and items for your character. Explore one of the many biomes and structures and craft torches for the underground caves. Each area offers new things for you to find!

Fight

Fight your way through hordes of enemies as the days past by. Weild up to 566 unique weapons split among four different classes for every playstyle. There is no limit to go against one of the 371 unique enemies as a melee, ranger, mage or summoner. The choice is yours.

Build

Build to your heart's content. From mineshafts, to treehouses, and to castles in the sky, you can be the builder you've always dreamt of. With 192 block types and the use of a trusty paintbrush - anything can be used to fulfill that creative desire.
